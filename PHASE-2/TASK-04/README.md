## Sensor Placement Notes

### DHT22
Mounted in open air and away from direct heat sources.

### HC-SR04
Mounted approximately 15–20 cm above the target surface.
Target kept perpendicular to sensor face.

### Soil Moisture Sensor
Capacitive Soil Moisture Sensor v1.2.
Probe inserted approximately 5 cm into soil.
Calibration performed using:
- Dry condition: ADC = 820
- Wet condition: ADC = 380

### LDR
Mounted facing ambient room lighting.
Tested under dark, ambient, and bright light conditions.
